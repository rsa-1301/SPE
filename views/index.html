About Us

SPE-VIT is one of its kind and a state-of-the-art in the niche of chapters in VIT, which projects the vision of a newly-found, yet much accomplished field of “liquid gold” in the university scenario, something which the students of VIT need to have an awareness about. For student members, we offer scholarships and fellowships for both undergraduate and graduate students pursuing degrees related to the oil and gas industry.
